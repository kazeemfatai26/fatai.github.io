About

Hi, I'm Adeoye. I hold a bachelor's degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. I have four year professional experience in Software research and development. I'm currently learning about Microservices, distributed systems and embedded applications.

I am interested in developing software and systems that focus on advanced human-computer interaction and integration and how these systems can be applied to healthcare, consumer products, and military applications to improve the quality of life.
