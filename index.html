About

Hi, I am Fatai - a First Class graduate of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. I have two years professional experience in data analysis and everything in between. I'm currently learning about cloud technologies, databases and machine learning application in power systems.

I am interested in developing models and systems that focus on advanced machine learning techniques and how it can be applied to power systems, energy demand forecasting, and renewable energy integration to improve the energy efficiency of the grid.
